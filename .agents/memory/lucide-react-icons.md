## Rule
Do not import `Github`, `Linkedin`, or `Twitter` from `lucide-react` — they were removed in v1.x.

**Why:** Brand icon guidelines prohibit their inclusion in open icon packs; lucide dropped them around v0.400+.

**How to apply:** Use these substitutes:
- `Github` → `Code2` or `GitBranch`
- `Linkedin` → `Briefcase`
- `Twitter` / `X` → `AtSign` or `MessageSquare`
